YouTube SEO Course

In this course, you will learn the strategies and techniques to optimize YouTube videos for better visibility and ranking in search results.

Course Details:

  • Duration: 6 weeks
  • Level: Beginner to Intermediate
  • Prerequisites: None

YouTube SEO Course Outline

  • Introduction to YouTube SEO
  • Keyword Research for YouTube
  • Optimizing Video Titles, Descriptions, and Tags
  • Creating Engaging Thumbnails
  • Increasing Watch Time and Audience Retention
  • Promoting Videos and Building Subscribers
  • Analytics and Tracking Performance
  • Optimizing YouTube Channel for Search
